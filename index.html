Frameworks for the borderless investor

The moving parts

Underneath every country playbook are the same recurring questions. These frameworks give you a repeatable way to think through each one, so a new jurisdiction becomes a checklist, not a research project from zero.

01

Residency & tax exposure

Where you're tax-resident, day-count thresholds, and how foreign income is treated where you live.

02

Brokerage & market access

Which brokers accept your residency, what markets you can reach, and where custody actually sits.

03

Structuring & entities

When holding personally is fine and when a company, trust or foundation earns its keep.

04

Banking & currency

Multi-currency accounts, moving money across borders, and managing FX without bleeding on spreads.

05

Reporting & compliance

CRS, FATCA, local filings: what has to be declared, to whom, and by when.

06

The technical stack

Tools, apps and workflows that make a location-independent portfolio actually manageable.
